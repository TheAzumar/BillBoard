Legends: Age of Chaos is a free-to-play love letter to the early days of Dark Ages with a focus on adventure, roleplay and community building. 

Be warned that intentionally abusing any bug for any reason may result in you character being deleted. If you are unsure what this means, please contact a member of the Legends Team for clarification.

Our community is small and we are counting on each other to help the game grow into something wonderful!

Please remember to stay safe, have fun and be kind to one another.
